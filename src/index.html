Welcome to the Portfolio of Truth & Josstice!

So, you're thinking, why Truth and Josstice?

Funny story really, a long long time ago in my twenties, I was gifted a Justice tour poster which had been modified to say Truth and Josstice! This became a few things for me, my online moniker, and a pretty appropriate explanation for how I operate in the wild wide world!

But what does that mean for me and my developer journey?

I am a life long learner, it is the eagerness for new skills and abilities that has drawn me to Web Development. This is a field that constantly expands and evolves. The search for knowledge and skills is the Truth in Truth and Josstice!

I aim to bring my personal flair, and joy of communication and inclusion to all the work that I do. I am socially conscious, and have worked with a wide variety of individuals and demographics in my previous work life. I want to share my joy of working with people with the digital world! Curiousity and compassion form the Josstice in Truth and Josstice!
